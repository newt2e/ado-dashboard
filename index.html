    Generate your Azure DevOps Personal Access Token (PAT)

    1. Open Azure DevOps: https://dev.azure.com/SmartQ-Global-Digital-Cafeteria-Suite/
    2. Sign in using your company account.
    3. Click the User Settings icon on the right side of the top bar — it shows a tooltip "User settings", just left of your profile picture.
    4. From the dropdown, click Personal access tokens.
    5. Click New Token.
    6. Enter a Name, select SmartQ-Global-Digital-Cafeteria-Suite as the Organisation, set Expiration to 30 days or longer. Under Scopes select Custom defined, scroll to Work Items and tick Read only.
    7. Click Create.
    8. Copy the token immediately — it is only shown once.
    9. Paste it into the PAT field in the dashboard.
    ⚠ The token is only shown once. Copy it before closing the ADO window.
